1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Aesthopie ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our AI-based products and services. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communication preferences
  • Customer support interactions

2.2 AI Training Data

When you use our AI products and services, we may collect:

  • Input data you provide to our AI systems
  • Generated outputs and responses
  • Usage patterns and interaction data
  • Performance metrics and feedback

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP addresses and device identifiers
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage statistics and analytics data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI products and services
  • AI Model Training: To train, improve, and optimize our AI algorithms (with appropriate safeguards)
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • Communication: To send you updates, newsletters, and important service notifications
  • Security: To protect our services and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. AI-Specific Privacy Practices

ü§ñ AI Data Handling

We implement special privacy measures for AI-related data processing to ensure your information is handled responsibly.

4.1 Data Anonymization

Before using your data for AI training purposes, we:

  • Remove or anonymize personally identifiable information
  • Implement data minimization principles
  • Use aggregated and de-identified datasets when possible

4.2 AI Output Responsibility

We take measures to ensure that our AI systems:

  • Do not reproduce or reveal personal information from training data
  • Are regularly audited for privacy compliance
  • Include safeguards against bias and discrimination

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers who assist in delivering our services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure data storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection best practices
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for specific processing activities

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside our primary jurisdiction, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
